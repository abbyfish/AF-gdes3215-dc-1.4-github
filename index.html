Grapefruit Crush

  • 2 oz of Deep Eddy's Grapefruit Vodka
  • 1 oz of Triple Sec
  • 1/2 of fresh squeezed Grapefruit juice
  • 2oz of club soda

Garnished with a fresh grapefruit and lime slice



How to make:

Start by squeezing half of a grapefruit with the juicer machine into a fresh glass. Next, take a jigger and fill it up with 2 ounces of Deep Eddy's Ruby Red Grapefruit Vodka. Follow that up with the ounce of triple sec, and then fill the rest of the glass with one cup of ice and club soda. Let the server garnish with the lime and grapefruit slice.
